Online Entry for Autumn Open

Saturday 3rd October 2026

  • Players who are members of IGolf and Open Play Golf are NOT eligible to enter. Any players entering will be removed and refunded.
  • For UK entrants your CDH Number is required at point of entry
  • Overseas Member and Overseas Visitor entries cannot be done online.  Please email to enter: mailto:reservations@broragolfclub.co.uk. For Overseas Entrants your Proof of Handicap Must be Shown on the Day
  • Tee times at point of entry may not be final as will endevour to make groups into 3/4 ball games depending on the competition format.
  • Refunds for withdrawals are available up until 5 days before the event
  • The use of a buggy is only permitted with advance notification of a medical certificate.
  • Tee times cannot be changed on the day of play.
  • Where a draw is done, tee times are final.
  • Consideration will be given to medical and travel requests received in advance.
This competition requires that fees be paid to complete entry. If the payment process is not completed, your entry may be removed.
